TARCURY Ads API

TARCURY Ads API is an internal Amazon Ads reporting and optimization system used by our company for our own Amazon advertising account.

Purpose

The system helps us retrieve advertising performance data, analyze campaign performance, monitor budgets, analyze keywords and search terms, and improve advertising efficiency and profitability.

Use of Amazon Ads API

This application is used only for our own direct advertiser account. We do not manage third-party advertiser accounts and do not provide advertising services to external clients.

Data Use

Advertising data is used only for internal reporting, budget analysis, keyword analysis, search term analysis, campaign optimization, and profit improvement.
